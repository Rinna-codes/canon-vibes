# What is Canon Vibes? 
You ever have your moments where you get hooked onto a fictional fandom or character, say maybe like superhero characters, and it takes over your entire life? Occupies your mind 24/7? If you know, you know. It's like this hyperfixation of creating curated Spotify playlists, creating Pinterest moods aesthetic mood boards, or even branching off into fanfic like A03 or Wattpad surrounding that particular fannfom or character. Why not bring those unique things together and create a web application for it? Canon Vibes is the place for that. 

**Why not build an application for people like me and anyone that has this special music playlist for a character or story they love and wish a place where that creative connection can live visually?**
